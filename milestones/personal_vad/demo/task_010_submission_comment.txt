I implemented a personal TinyVAD evaluation workflow in `milestones/personal_vad/scripts/` with three scripts: `run_my_experiment.py`, `tune_energy_vad.py`, and `plot_model_vs_energy.py`. The demo shows live execution on TORGO samples, comparison metrics (MSE/correlation), model footprint evidence (size and parameter count), and generated qualitative plots. I also explain the frame-rate alignment challenge and my interpolation-based solution used to compare student and baseline predictions on a shared timeline.
